About Framed by Sixteen28

At Framed by Sixteen28, we believe that eyewear is more than just a visual aid—it's a statement of personal style and an extension of your identity. Our dedicated team of eyewear consultants combines fashion expertise with technical knowledge to help you find frames that not only complement your face shape and lifestyle but also express your unique personality.

With our modern frames and classic appeal, we bridge the gap between contemporary design and timeless elegance, ensuring that your eyewear remains stylish regardless of changing trends.
